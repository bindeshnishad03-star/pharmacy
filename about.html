WHO WE ARE

Pioneering Healthcare Innovations With Quality Assurance

Satik Pharmaceuticals Pvt. Ltd. was founded with a singular focus: to manufacture and distribute premium pharmaceutical products that improve community health. Based in Jaunpur, Uttar Pradesh, we have emerged as a highly respected name in generic drug formulation, contract packaging, and third-party manufacturing services.

We work closely with hospitals, clinics, national drug agencies, and local pharmacy stores. Our team of experienced chemists and quality control experts ensure that every medicine we release matches global standards of bio-availability and therapeutic effectiveness.

INNOVATION & STANDARDS

State-of-the-Art Formulation Testing Laboratories

At Satik Pharmaceuticals, innovation is guided by scientific validation. Our research and development desk continuously refines drug formulations to ensure higher stability, reduced side effects, and enhanced efficacy.

Our quality control (QC) labs operate round-the-clock, conducting rigorous chromatography tests, moisture content analysis, and dissolution profiling. We are dedicated to maintaining standard clinical trials and complying with international standards set by the CDSCO and WHO.

  • Advanced chemical & microbiological assay testing
  • FDA & DCGI approved medicine formulations
  • Eco-friendly clean waste disposal and safety protocols
