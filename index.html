Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to general questions about partnering with Satik Pharmaceuticals.

What pharmaceutical products do you offer?

▾

We manufacture a wide range of DCGI-approved formulations, including Tablets, Capsules, Liquid Syrups, Injections, Antibiotics, Pain Relievers, Pediatric Suspensions, Dermatology Ointments, and Ayurvedic formulations.

Do you provide third-party manufacturing?

▾

Yes, we offer comprehensive third-party contract manufacturing services. Partners can leverage our WHO-GMP compliant facilities, custom packaging capabilities, and full regulatory documentation support for their custom brand formulations.

How can distributors partner with you?

▾

Distributors can apply for monopoly rights under our PCD Pharma Franchise program. We supply promotional visual aids, catalogs, free physician samples, and ethical marketing guidelines to support regional growth.

What quality standards do you follow?

▾

All our manufacturing facilities adhere to strict WHO and GMP (Good Manufacturing Practice) standards. We perform raw material testing, in-process checking, and finished batch stability analysis inside our dedicated QA/QC laboratories.

How do I place an order or request quotes?

▾

You can send an inquiry directly through our contact form, email us at satikpharmaceuticals@gmail.com, or use the quick product inquiry modal. Our corporate desk will draft quotes and dispatch franchise terms within 24 hours.
